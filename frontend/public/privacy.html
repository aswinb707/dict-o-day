Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Welcome to Dict-o-Day. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized learning experience, we collect the following information when you create an account:

  • Account Credentials: Username, Email address, and a securely hashed password.
  • Personal Information: Date of birth (collected solely to enforce minimum age requirements of 10+ years).
  • Learning Preferences: Selected vocabulary difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard) and daily learning goals.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users and manage access to your account.
  • To customize and generate your personalized daily vocabulary words.
  • To keep track of your daily learning sessions and streak counts.
  • To enforce safety policies, including our minimum age requirement of 10 years.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take security seriously and implement appropriate safeguards to protect your personal data:

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard cryptography (BCrypt) before being stored in our database.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.
  • We only retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active.

4. Children's Privacy

Dict-o-Day requires users to be at least 10 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 10. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 10, we will delete that information immediately.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, correct, or request the deletion of your account and personal information at any time. To do so, please manage your profile through the app settings or contact us directly.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or services. We will notify you of any major changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
